About Us


From very humble beginning around the year 1998; BR Edutech transformed slowly into a fully professional education provider!

Since 2012 the facilities have been upgraded to meet the latest needs of students in imparting quality education! BR Edutech endures with very close association with all levels of students and uses technology,latest learning methodologies and most motivating guidance to bring out the best in its students.
